Welcome to Tunisian Food Delights

Explore the rich flavors of Tunisia

🫓 Couscous

The national dish of Tunisia, served with vegetables, chickpeas, and your choice of meat or fish. Rich in spices like harissa and cumin.

🌶️ Brik

A delicious deep-fried pastry filled with egg, tuna, capers, and parsley. Crunchy outside, soft and savory inside.

🥘 Lablabi

A warm chickpea soup served with olive oil, garlic, cumin, and harissa. Typically eaten with pieces of stale bread—perfect for a comforting meal.

🥗 Mechouia Salad

A grilled vegetable salad made with tomatoes, peppers, onions, and garlic, topped with tuna, eggs, and olives.

🐟 Ojja with Merguez

Spicy tomato and egg dish cooked with Merguez sausage—a popular Tunisian street food!
